Instructions

Welcome to the Number Guessing Game! 🎮

Your objective is to guess a randomly selected number between 1 and 50.

How to Play:

  • You have a maximum of 5 attempts to guess the correct number.
  • After each guess, you'll receive feedback:
    • If your guess is too low, you'll see "Your guess is too low."
    • If your guess is too high, you'll see "Your guess is too high."
    • If you guess correctly, you win!
  • After 5 unsuccessful attempts, the game will reveal the correct number.

Tips:

  • Start with a number around the middle (25) to narrow down the possibilities quickly.
  • Pay attention to the hints after each guess!
  • Have fun and good luck! 🍀
